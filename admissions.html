Admission Procedure

OPEN CATEGORY

30% seats are filled through common entrance test (EAMCET) Conducted by Government of Andhra Pradesh

MANAGEMENT QUOTA

15% seats are filled Under NRI Quota and National Integration Quota. NRI Seats are fill based on minimum of 60% marks attained in Intermediate Examination OR based on EAMCET rank of the Candidate. National Integration Quota seats are based AIEE rank / 60% marks in qualifying examination.

CRITERIA FOR ADMISSIONS

As per Government of Andhra Pradesh norms, decided by AP Government Year to Year. The Government of Andhra Pradesh fix the minimum cut off marks depending upon number of candidates qualified. For 2006-2007 Academic year minimum cut off Percentage is 30% in EAMCET.
